Research Participation — Informed Consent

You are invited to participate in a scientific study. Before proceeding, please consider the following information:

In this study, you will be shown a detailed description of a procedure that was completed by other Prolific participants as part of an experiment. You will be asked to estimate how long each part of the procedure took those participants, on average, to complete.

You will see a process map that breaks the procedure into steps and may reveal additional details about the actions involved.

There are no risks involved in this study. Your privacy will be fully respected, and all data will be collected and analysed anonymously.

Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

About the experiment

In a recent experiment on Prolific, participants completed a fictional online government application. Your task is to estimate how long it took them.

What participants did: They role-played as a fictional resident of "Newland" and completed an online application for a Municipal Green Zone Vehicle Access Permit — a permit required to drive in restricted low-emission zones.

The application required them to provide personal details, assess their eligibility, enter vehicle information from official documents, and submit supporting documentation.
